ABOUT US

"Education is the most poweful weapon you can use to change the world" - Nelson Mandela

There are many ways we as humans can be impactful in the world we live in, there is no greater impact

than spreading education, empowering people who will empower and teach people, who in turn will empower and

teach more. Education is the first step for people to gain knowledge, critical thinking empowerment and skills they need

to make the world a better place. We at Orphans2School believe every

one deserves an opportunity to education, so we make it our priority to make a difference however we can.


MISSION STATEMENT

Reaching out to maximum number of deprived children and bringing about

Sustainable changes in their lives through education

Every child's dream is valid!!!

Building a future in which every child has an opportunity to access excellent

quality education, aworld where every child can dare to dream,

without finacial restriction hindering them
